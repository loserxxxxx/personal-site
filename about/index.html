ABOUT SEMATARY
butcher house. 808s. poison.

Sematary, is an American rapper, songwriter, and producer from Northern California. He began releasing music and founded the Haunted Mound rap collective in 2019.

The debut mixtape Grave House (2019) was the start — raw sounding witch house, Rainbow Bridge 3 (2021) expanded the sound. Butcher House (2023) was the biggest year yet singles pouring out constantly, With Known collabs with Hackle, Buckshot, and Black Kray.

2026: still going dumb. RAGING WOLF out now.
